The value of the Internet of Things market in Poland in 2019 amounted to $2.2 billion, i.e. by 6.4 percent more than a year earlier, according to the report of the Digital Poland Association. It added that the law that does not meet the needs is currently a barrier to the development of the Internet of Things. The report indicates that the Internet of Things, in combination with smartphones, telecommunications networks, IT systems, software, and machines, allows for the creation of smart homes, work and life places (smart cities) and contributes to the global technological change (smart world) every person participates. On average, there are almost three IoT devices connected to the network per person.
